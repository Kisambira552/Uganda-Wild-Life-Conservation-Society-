Discover Uganda’s Wildlife

Mammals

Africa's largest land mammal, the elephant, roams freely across Uganda's national parks.

Birds

The grey crowned crane is Uganda's national bird, symbolizing the beauty of Uganda’s avian life.

Reptiles

Uganda's rivers and lakes are home to the dangerous Nile crocodile, a top predator in the region.

Endangered Species

Mountain gorillas, critically endangered, are protected in Uganda’s Bwindi Impenetrable Forest.

Our Conservation Projects

Gorilla Protection Project

We are working to protect the endangered mountain gorillas by creating safe habitats and reducing poaching.
